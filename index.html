Intersting Facts About Camels

  • Camels eyes have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to prevent sand to enter their eyes
  • Camels hump consists of stored fat which they can metabolise when food and water are scarce
  • The thick pads of skin on their chest and knees so that camels can comfortably sit in very hot sand.

Intersting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Intersting Facts About Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas are oval-shaped, winged insects that provide a buzzing and clicking song heard in nature throughout the summer
  • Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source
  • They spend most of their lives underground as larvae

Intersting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds
  • They can recognize all 26 letters od the english language!!
  • They have an exceptional eyesight and ability to identify objects on a distance of 26 miles
  • They have a very sensitive sense of hearing so they are able to detect distance storms,earthquakes ad volcanic eruptions
